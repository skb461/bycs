KNOW ABOUT BYCS

Welcome to Bangladesh Youth Climate School.

Youth Career Institute is a youth-based organization that works for the career development of youth across the country. Every year, 66% of our country's total graduates remain unemployed. YCI is working on lowering the unemployment rate through developing a skilled and self-empowered youth community. Since 2019, YCI has organized many workshops, trainings, and national events, and each year brings new activities to the list.
